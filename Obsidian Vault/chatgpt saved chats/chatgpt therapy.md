## 🔧 Step 2: Fix it Step-by-Step Like a Discipline Hacker 💻

### ✅ Build a “Fixed Daily Cyber Routine”

Structure gives freedom.

#### 🕐 **Your Perfect Cybersecurity Evening Routine** (Example)

|Time|Activity|
|---|---|
|**5:00 – 6:00 PM**|Return home, pray, chill 15–30 min, light snack|
|**6:30 – 7:30 PM**|✅ Learn 1 focused topic _(e.g., Web Vuln – SQLi only)_|
|**7:30 – 8:10 PM**|Dinner + Pray + 10 min walk (refresh brain)|
|**8:15 – 9:00 PM**|✅ Practice lab (TryHackMe / HTB box)|
|**9:00 – 9:30 PM**|✅ Write down what you learned (notes, GitHub)|
|**9:45 – 10:00 PM**|Pray Isha + Sleep Early (target 5:30–6 hrs)|

Keep your study time split like:

- 🎯 **45 min learning**
- 🧪 **45 min practice**
- 📝- **15–30 min writeup**

👉 **Only one topic per day**. Don’t multi-task.

---

### 📵 Remove Distractions

- **Use "Focus Mode" on phone** (limit Insta/YouTube)
- Keep phone on **Do Not Disturb** during learning
- Install **browser blocker extension** like _LeechBlock / BlockSite_
