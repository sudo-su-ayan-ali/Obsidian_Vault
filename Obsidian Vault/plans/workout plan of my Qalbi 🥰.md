## 🌸 Important notes before starting

- **No heavy calorie restriction** (very important for hormones)
    
- Sleep 7–9 hours
    
- Drink water
    
- Stop if you feel dizzy or pain (not normal muscle burn)
    

---

# 🗓️ WEEKLY HOME WORKOUT PLAN

### ⏱️ Daily time: 30–45 minutes

### 🏠 Equipment: Yoga mat (optional), chair, water bottle

---

## **DAY 1 – Full Body + Hormone-Friendly Cardio**

**Warm-up (5–7 min)**

- March in place – 1 min
    
- Arm circles – 30 sec
    
- Hip circles – 30 sec
    
- Jumping jacks (slow) – 1 min
    
- Neck & shoulder rolls
    

**Workout**

- Bodyweight squats – 3 × 12
    
- Knee push-ups or wall push-ups – 3 × 10
    
- Glute bridges – 3 × 15
    
- Standing shoulder taps – 3 × 20 taps
    
- Step-backs lunges – 3 × 10 each leg
    

**Light cardio**

- Brisk walking in place – 5 min
    

**Cool down**

- Stretch legs, hips, shoulders – 5 min
    

---

## **DAY 2 – Lower Body + Core**

**Warm-up**

- Leg swings
    
- Hip openers
    
- Easy squats
    

**Workout**

- Squats – 3 × 15
    
- Reverse lunges – 3 × 12
    
- Wall sit – 3 × 30 sec
    
- Glute kickbacks – 3 × 15 each side
    

**Core**

- Dead bug – 3 × 10
    
- Plank (knees okay) – 3 × 20–30 sec
    

**Stretch**

- Hamstrings, glutes, hips
    

---

## **DAY 3 – Active Recovery + Hormone Balance**

(No intense workout today 🌿)

Choose **ONE**:

- 30–40 min **brisk walk**
    
- OR **Yoga / stretching**
    

**Yoga poses for hormone balance**

- Butterfly pose
    
- Child’s pose
    
- Cat–Cow
    
- Cobra
    
- Legs up the wall (2–3 min)
    

Finish with **5 minutes of deep breathing**

---

## **DAY 4 – Upper Body + Core**

**Warm-up**

- Arm swings
    
- Shoulder rolls
    
- Light jumping jacks
    

**Workout**

- Wall or knee push-ups – 3 × 10–12
    
- Chair triceps dips – 3 × 10
    
- Arm circles (small → big) – 3 × 30 sec
    
- Superman hold – 3 × 20 sec
    

**Core**

- Crunches – 3 × 12
    
- Heel taps – 3 × 20
    
- Side plank – 2 × 15 sec each side
    

---

## **DAY 5 – Full Body Flow + Light Cardio**

**Circuit (repeat 3 rounds)**

- Squats – 12
    
- Marching high knees – 30 sec
    
- Glute bridges – 15
    
- Standing punches – 30 sec
    
- Bird dog – 10 each side
    

Rest 60 sec between rounds

**Stretch**

- Full body stretches
    

---

## **DAY 6 – Dance / Fun Cardio 💃**

- 20–30 min **dance workout**, Zumba, or free movement
    
- Keep it fun, not exhausting
    

Finish with stretching + breathing

---

## **DAY 7 – Rest & Reset 🌸**

- Gentle stretching
    
- Walk if you want
    
- Deep breathing
    
- Relax
    

Rest days are **essential for hormones**

---

# 🍎 Bonus: Hormone-Friendly Tips (FOR YOU only 🥰)

- Eat **enough food** (don’t starve)
    
- Include:
    
    - Protein (eggs, dal, paneer, yogurt, chicken)
        
    - Healthy fats (nuts, seeds, ghee, olive oil)
        
    - Fruits & vegetables
        
- Avoid:
    
    - Skipping meals
        
    - Excessive HIIT
        
    - Over-exercising
