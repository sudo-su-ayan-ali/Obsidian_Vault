- **The AI "Elevation" Struggle:** Companies have moved past just "testing" AI. The problem now is scaling it. Many industries are finding that while AI can write code or draft reports, it can’t replace human intuition. There is a massive **skills gap**—everyone wants AI experts, but few want to train the current workforce to become them.
- 
```
Burnout → People leave the field
    ↓
Remaining team is understaffed
    ↓
More pressure on fewer people
    ↓
More burnout → More people leave
    ↓
Organizations rely MORE on AI (which they don't fully understand)
    ↓
Institutional knowledge is LOST
    ↓
When AI fails, nobody knows how to respond manually
```
